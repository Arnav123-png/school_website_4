About Us


Ms. Anju Sharma

Principal

A competent, versatile and knowledgeable individual with 15 years of experience in teaching. She holds a M.A. in Economics along with a B.Ed. degree. She believes in the right blend of traditional and modern education techniques, with a strong value system so that students develop into conscientious human beings.



Ms.Supriya Prasad

Headmistress

An expert in the field of education with 15 years of experience as a teacher, principal and now headmistress, she holds double major in psychology and English, along with a bachelor's degree in education. Passionate about teaching, she strives to lead the school, challenge and support the staff, and motivate children to be the best they can be.
